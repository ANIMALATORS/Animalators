🎮 Movement & Camera
WASD
Move your animal. Direction is relative to the camera — W always runs forward from your view.
🖱️
Move the mouse to rotate the camera around your animal. Moving mouse left/right orbits the camera. Moving up/down adjusts the pitch angle.
Click
Basic attack in the direction your animal is facing. Hold to attack repeatedly.
💡 Your animal always faces the direction the camera is looking — so aim with your mouse, then attack!
Page 2 of 4
⚡ Abilities
Q
Attack Ability — your main combat move. Shorter cooldown, used often. Each animal has a unique Q ability.
E
Special Power — your most powerful move. Longer cooldown. Can be AoE, a charge, a mega strike, etc.
F
Defence / Utility — survival move. Regen, invisibility, armor buff, speed boost — depends on your animal.
💡 Watch the bottom HUD — abilities show READY in gold when available. The ring fills as cooldown counts down.
Page 3 of 4
☢️ Zone & Survival
☢️
The Zone closes over time. Stay inside the glowing orange dashed circle or you take heavy damage. The minimap compass (top-right) always shows where the safe zone is.
❤️
Your HP bar is at the bottom centre. When it hits zero, you're eliminated. Some abilities and upgrades give passive regen.
⬆️
Kill enemies to earn XP and level up mid-game — each level gives a random buff (more damage, HP, speed, etc).
💡 Bots have limited vision — sneak behind cover and they won't see you! They remember your last position for 3 seconds.
Page 4 of 4
🪙 Coins & Upgrades
🪙
Kill enemies to earn coins. Kill streaks give bonus coins. Coins carry over between games permanently.
⬆️
Spend coins in the Upgrades shop on the main menu. Upgrades are permanent: more HP, speed, damage, armor, cooldown reduction, regen, and more.
🎲
You get 3 random animals to pick from before each match. You can reroll once per game. Pick based on your playstyle — tank, assassin, hunter, brawler.
🔥 Pro tip: The Vengeance upgrade deals more damage the lower your HP — combine it with a tanky animal for insane late-game damage!
