🗺️ New Map
🌿 Deep Jungle Night — Pitch dark arena lit only by bioluminescent trees, glowing mushroom landmarks, and toxic green pools. Dense fog. No sunlight. Creepiest map yet.
✨ New Features
XP + Level Up — Earn XP from kills. Level up mid-game for a random buff (damage, HP, speed, armor, regen, range). A gold banner shows on screen when you level up.
Environmental Hazards — Lava pools burn (18 dmg/s), ice patches slow movement, toxic pools in nightjungle poison (8 dmg/s). All visible on the ground.
Ambient particles — Embers on volcano, snow on arctic/tundra, green spores in nightjungle, dust on city. All rendered around your position.
New upgrade: Vengeance 🩸 — Deal up to +32% bonus damage the lower your HP. Up to 4 levels.
Per-map coloured accent lights — Green glow in nightjungle, neon on city buildings (4 colours), lava glow on volcano, blue shimmer on arctic. Every map has unique lighting.
City street lights now emit actual light — point lights on every lamp post and neon building.
